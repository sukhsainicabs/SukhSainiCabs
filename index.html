All India Tourist Permit

Our vehicles hold valid All India Tourist Permits — legally operating across every state in India without any border restriction.

🛡️ What is All India Permit?

  • Legal permission to run taxi service across all Indian states
  • Issued by Regional Transport Authority (RTA), Punjab
  • Valid for interstate travel — no checkpoint issues at borders
  • Mandatory for long-distance & tourist cab services
  • Covers all 28 States + 8 Union Territories of India

✅ Your Benefit as a Passenger

  • No police trouble at any state border crossing
  • Full legal protection for you and your driver
  • Insured vehicle travel across all of India
  • Professional, verified, experienced drivers only
  • Complete peace of mind on long journeys

📋 Our Permit Details

  • Type: All India Tourist Vehicle Permit
  • Authority: Punjab Regional Transport Office
  • Base Office: Fazilka, Punjab, India
  • Coverage: Nationwide — all states & union territories
  • All papers regularly renewed & always valid
